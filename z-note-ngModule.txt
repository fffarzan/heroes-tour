>> metadata:
    1-Angular needs to know how the pieces of your application fit together and what 
    other files and libraries the app requires.
    2-a) some of their are in `@component` decorators.
      b) some of their are in `@ngModule` decorators.

>> AppModule class: The Angular CLI generated an AppModule class in 
`src/app/app.module.ts` when it created the project.

>> ngModule: 
    1-Every component must be declared in exactly one `NgModule`.
